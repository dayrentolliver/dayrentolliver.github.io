About

I began my coding journey in January of 2016, during my senior year of college at West Texas A&M University. I decided to take an intro to programming class on a whim, based on a recommendation from a friend. I quickly fell in love with the idea that I could create anything I wanted with a *few* lines of code.


Since graduating with a B.B.A. in Marketing, I have always found my way back to coding. I recently made the decision to pursue it as a career. I'm looking to join an organization that's willing to invest in junior developers, like me.


In May of 2019, I began volunteering as a Teacher's Assistant with RefCode, an organization that teaches refugees how to build their own website in 10 weeks, using HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. In the short time that I've been volunteering with the organization, it's been really encouraging to see how excited the students are about building their very first website!
