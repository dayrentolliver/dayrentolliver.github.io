About

Hello, my name is Dayren Tolliver. I began my journey into programming, during my senior year of college at West Texas A&M University, in January of 2016. I decided to take an intro to programming class on a whim, based on a recommendation from a friend. I quickly fell in love with the idea that I code create anything I wanted with a few lines of code.

Since graduating with a B.B.A. in Marketing, I have always found my way back to coding. I recently made the decision to pursue it as a career. I'm looking to join an organization that's willing to invest in junior developers, like me.

Finally, I recently began volunteering, as a TA, with RefCode, an organization that teaches refugees how to build their own website in 10 weeks. In the short time that I've been volunteering with the organization, it's been really exciting to see how excited the students are about creating their very first website!
